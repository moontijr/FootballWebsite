Steaua Bucharest Wins the Romanian Cup

Steaua Bucharest has won the Romanian Cup for the first time since 2015, after defeating Universitatea Craiova 1-0 in the final. The victory also means that Steaua has secured a spot in next season's Europa League.

Steaua manager Costel Gâlcă praised his team's effort and mentality, saying, "This is a fantastic achievement for the club and for the players. We worked hard all season and we deserved this cup. Now we need to focus on our next challenge, which is the league title."

Dinamo Bucharest Avoids Relegation

Dinamo Bucharest has avoided relegation to the second division after defeating FC Argeș Pitești 2-1 in the final matchday of the season. The victory means that Dinamo finished in 13th place, one point ahead of FC Argeș Pitești.

Dinamo manager Adrian Mutu expressed his relief and joy after the match, saying, "This was a very difficult season for us, but we fought until the end and we managed to stay in Liga 1. I am proud of my players and I am confident that we will come back stronger next season."
